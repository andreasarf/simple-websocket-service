Based on an analysis of color theory principles for effective visual communication, I suggest a triadic color scheme to create a balanced yet visually engaging poster. Consider using a dominant, calming color like a dusty blue ($#6A8D92$) for the background or large graphical elements to establish a professional tone. Pair this with a vibrant, warm terracotta ($#E27D60$) for key information or calls-to-action, as this complementary warmth will draw the eye without being jarring. Finally, use a pale cream or off-white ($#F7F3E3$) for your primary text to ensure maximum readability and provide a clean, harmonious foundation that ties the other two colors together effectively. This combination is designed to be both aesthetically pleasing and strategically effective in guiding a viewer's attention.
